Four Ways In. Zero Downloads + Full CLI.

SSH, QUIC, Browser, and UDP — four distinct transport methods in one platform. Choose the zero-download path or unlock advanced features with our CLI binary.

01

SSH One-Liner

Works on any machine with a terminal. No download, no signup, no binary. Every OS since 2005 has SSH built in. Just run one command and you're live.

ssh -p 443 -R0:localhost:3000 token@tunl...

02

CLI Binary (QUIC)

Download the zyppr binary for the fastest possible experience. QUIC transport (UDP-based), auto-reconnect, config-as-code, and P2P mesh.

zyppr tunnel 3000 --udp 25565

Browser Tunnel

Open your browser, paste your token, and your localhost is live. Supports HTTP, streaming responses, and SSE. Nothing to install at all.

browser → paste token → live

Built-in Features

Every Feature You Need.

Not plugins. Not add-ons. Every feature below is production-ready and included in your plan. 29 features. One platform.

Security & Protection

E2E Encryption

Every tunnel is encrypted end-to-end with visual verification. Each session gets a unique emoji fingerprint (🚀🌊💎🔑) so you can visually confirm no one is intercepting your traffic.

AI Gateway World-First

Real-time threat detection. Blocks SQL injection, XSS, specific paths, and malicious bots automatically.

Developer Tools

Request Inspector

See every request hitting your tunnel — headers, body, timing, response code. Full visibility.

Request Replay

Modify headers, body, or method of any captured request and replay it instantly with one click.

Auto-Swagger

Zyppr observes your live traffic and auto-generates OpenAPI documentation by usage.

DB Studio Exclusive

A built-in web SQL browser for your tunneled databases. Browse schemas, explore tables, and run read-only queries from your dashboard. Supports Postgres, MySQL, SQLite.

Traffic Snapshots

Capture point-in-time traffic logs to disk. Share these snapshots with your team for collaborative debugging.

Advanced Networking

Custom Domains + Auto-SSL

Bring your own domain. Point a CNAME record, and Zyppr automatically provisions a Let's Encrypt TLS certificate. HTTPS on your custom domain in minutes, auto-renewed forever.

Webhook Breakpoints

Pause an incoming webhook mid-flight. Inspect or modify it. Then release it to your server.
