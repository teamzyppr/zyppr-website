Design Philosophy

Why LatticeWire Exists

OpenVPN is flexible but slow. IPsec is incredibly complex. WireGuard is fast but rigid. Modern networking requires more.

Performance First

Zero-allocation packet processing with lock-free buffer pooling. Deterministic high throughput even across trans-atlantic connections.

Cryptographic Future-Proofing

Hybrid ML-KEM-768 + X25519 key exchange. Prevents "harvest now, decrypt later" quantum attacks natively.

Stealth Transport

Dual UDP and TCP modes bypass DPI firewalls. Shadow Port Authorization gates prevent unauthorized scanning.
